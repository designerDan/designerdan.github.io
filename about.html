FAQs

How long will this take and how much will it cost me?

The timeline and cost depend on the complexity of the project. After an initial discussion of your needs, I'll provide a timeline and cost breakdown tailored to your specific situation.

What deliverables can we expect at each stage of the project?

You'll receive wireframes or low-fidelity prototypes early on, followed by high-fidelity designs and clickable prototypes. Depending on your needs, I may also provide technical diagrams like user flows, user personas, or usability reports.

How do you measure the success of the design after launch?

I consider the business and other stakeholder metrics of success, but from a pure UX standpoint, measuring the success of the UX, I use the bulletproof industry standard UMUXLite survey. It is only two questions. There's no reason not to use it.

How do you handle feedback and revisions during the design process?

I set up structured reviews for gathering user and stakeholder feedback. I consider the feedback in light of project and business goals which guides the revisions to be made to reach those goals.

How do you ensure the design aligns with our business goals?

I work closely with stakeholders to define clear objectives and KPIs upfront. Throughout the design process, I continuously refer back to these goals, ensuring my design decisions work for and not against them.

What is your process for conducting user research?

I start from where you are and what you're looking to learn. Where beneficial, I'll analyze existing data for trends and patterns and provide a research method to fill any knowledge gaps. This keeps my design decisions informed and grounded in real user experiences.
